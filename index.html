Tip: Recall rejected takeoff commands.
Active Operational Warning Deck
INCORRECT: "We want to reject the takeoff because of fire" (Vague intention)
CORRECT phraseology: "Stopping" / "Rejecting takeoff" (Immediate state declaration)
INCORRECT: "The altitude of the plane was changed by the pilot" (Wordy passive)
CORRECT passive: "Altitude was adjusted to flight level 240." (Concise procedural voice)
