Speaking Templates

Reusable opening sentences. Adapt one to any picture.

1 — Normal operation

This picture depicts a normal airport operation. I can see a commercial aircraft on the apron. The situation looks normal and well controlled.

2 — Abnormal / emergency

This picture depicts an abnormal aviation situation. An aircraft appears to be involved in a serious incident. The situation may be dangerous and requires attention from the crew or airport services.

3 — Runway incident

This picture depicts a runway incident. One aircraft appears to be on or near the runway. This may create a safety risk if separation is not maintained.

4 — Aftermath of an incident

This picture depicts the aftermath of an aviation incident. The aircraft appears to have sustained visible damage. Emergency personnel may be securing the area.
